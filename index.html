Our Story

A Kitchen Born from Grandmother's Recipes

Parigai began with a simple belief: the most nourishing food is the food your grandmother made — measured not in milligrams but in love. We opened our doors in 2005 in Chennai with a menu of 12 items and a promise to never compromise on ingredients.

Today, we've grown — but our batter is still ground fresh every morning, our chutneys still made in small batches, and our dosa tawa still seasoned the old way. We've simply added more healthy, organic, and millet-based options to serve every kind of body and preference.
